How To Play

A posistion on a Valorant map will be displayed, correctly identifiing the location will result in a win. Upon answering incorrectly, you will be prompted further with additional details from your answer.

A green box indicates the the letter is in the word and in the correct spot.

A yellow box indicates that the letter is in the word but in the incorrect spot.

A grey box indicates that the letter is not in the word.
 

The prefix refers to the site location of the callout, these are limited to a,b,c and mid. These will not change color until the word is solved.

The location is the main portion of the callout, each of these spaces will change color.

A new location will appear everyday, good luck!
